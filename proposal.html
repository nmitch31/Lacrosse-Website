The History and Evolution of Lacrosse

  • My website is going to be about the history and evolution of lacrosse
  • This site will be important firstly because lacrosse has had a big role in my life and learning more about the history of the game that has impacted me so much will be beneficial for me. Also, it has been becoming more popular in recent years, so educating newcomers and people trying to get more into the sport will be beneficial for the whole lacrosse community! My objective is to educate people on how the oldest sport in America and fastest game on two feet has come to be what it is now.
  • My target audience will be lacrosse players, lacrosse fans, and hopefully the sports community as a whole.
  • I am planning on having a page dedicated to the creation of the game by the Native Americans, a page dedicated to the sport's evolution and how it came to be today, a page on the lacrosse stick, what it means and how it has changed over time, a page about the gear and gear technology, and lastly a page on the rules of each level, and how college lacrosse and professional lacrosse both play a big role in the sport.
